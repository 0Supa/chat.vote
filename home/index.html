chat.vote/vts

A VTube Studio plugin that allows your viewers to control your model.

It allows you to set up chat commands that trigger a VTube Studio hotkey.

Platform specific details:

After you login with your Google account the site will find your YouTube channel and connect to your live stream chat and start listening for viewer commands. If you have more than 1 active/upcoming stream then you will prompted to select which live chat to connect to.

All your data will be stored locally on your browser only.


chat.vote's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Privacy policy: chat.vote/pp

Twitch users can setup additional triggers for channel points rewards, subscription, sub gifting, and cheering.
